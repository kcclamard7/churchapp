DESCRIPTION: 

The application helps automate the management of the members of the organization, tithes, and offerings. The application will also help the treasurer to print invoice, receipt, and others important statement. The software will provide an overview of the entire financial state of the organization.
	
SOME FEATURES:

•	Log in to the system and log out securely
•	Create, update and delete user
•	Create, update and delete tithes, offerings and special contribution
•	Track expenses and display overall accounting balance
•	Generate reports based on a specific date
